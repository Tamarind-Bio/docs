## De Novo Miniprotein Design
  
De novo Miniprotein design involves creating new protein binders from scratch to target specific molecules or surfaces.

### Recommended Tools
- [**RFdiffusion**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/rfdiffusion)
- [**Bindcraft**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/bindcraft)

## Antibody CDR Design

Antibody CDR (Complementarity Determining Region) design focuses on engineering the variable regions of antibodies to improve binding affinity, specificity, and developability.

### Recommended Tools
- [**RFantibody**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/rfantibody)
- [**Igdesign**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/igdesign)
- [**Antifold**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/antifold)

## Interface Scaffolding

Interface scaffolding involves designing protein scaffolds to present functional motifs or binding interfaces in specific orientations.

### Recommended Tools
- [**RFdiffusion**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/rfdiffusion): with motif scaffolding task
- [**ProteinMPNN**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/proteinmpnn): For sequence design

## Binder Optimization

Optimizing existing protein binders involves improving their binding affinity, specificity, and stability through targeted mutations and structural modifications.

### Recommended Tools
- [**RFdiffusion**](https://app.tamarind.bio/protein-design) - Partial diffusion or binder redesign task
- [**ProteinMPNN**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/proteinmpnn)

## Improving Stability

Improving protein stability involves modifying sequences and structures to enhance thermal stability, resistance to aggregation, and overall robustness.

### Recommended Tools
- [**ThermoMPNN**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/thermompnn)
- [**ProteinMPNN-ddG**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/proteinmpnn-ddg)

## Solubilizing membrane proteins

Engineering a target membrane protein to be soluble without disrupting its function

### Recommended Tools
- [**ProteinMPNN with Soluble Weights**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/proteinmpnn)
- [**RFdiffusion**](https://app.tamarind.bio/protein-design)
- [**FixBB**](https://app.tamarind.bio/tools/fixbb)
